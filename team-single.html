About me

At Milkofarm, our journey began with a simple mission — to deliver fresh, pure, and ethical dairy straight from our farms to your home. With deep roots in traditional farming and a passion for natural living, we’ve created a space where cows are cared for with compassion, and milk is delivered with integrity. Our commitment to quality goes beyond just product — it's a reflection of our values, our people, and our promise to you.

My services

At Milkofarm, we offer a range of farm-fresh dairy services designed to bring natural goodness straight to your doorstep. From daily home delivery of pure cow and buffalo milk to customized subscription plans, every service is crafted with quality, convenience, and care in mind.

  • Daily doorstep delivery of farm-fresh milk, ensuring unmatched purity and taste in every drop.
  • Tailored subscription plans to suit your family's needs, with flexible options and easy management.
  • All-natural dairy products like ghee, curd, and paneer made using traditional, chemical-free methods.
